Sinistea Cafe

How to Play

You did it! You quit your office job and pursued your life long dream of opening a cafe.

Running this place is no easy task however, and there's talk of a mischevious witch running around town.

Below are all the instructions you'll need to make sure you keep your dream a reality!

Serving Customers

The main way you make money and score points in this game is by serving customers

Taking Orders

The image above each customer shows what they want to eat. The percentage bar beneath each customer shows how much time you have left to prepare the dish.

If time runs out before you complete the order, the customer leaves and your prestige is reduced.

Preparing Order

To create a dish, click on the three ingredients required to create the dish. The order of selected ingredients doesn't matter.

Dish Ingredients
Cookie Eggs + Flour + Chocolate
Hot Cocoa Sugar + Chocolate + Milk
Fruit Tart Fruit + Milk + Sugar
Strawberry Cake Flour + Milk + Fruit

Completing Order

Once you've selected all 3 ingredients, click the oven icon. If you've selected the correct combination of items, the empty plate will switch to whichever dish you created.

Now click on the customer who wanted that dish. They will disappear from the line, and your score, prestige, and coins will increase.

If you messed up the order, clicking the Trashcan will deselect any selected ingredients and clear the plate if you completed a recipe.

Upkeep

No one said this would be easy. Running a cafe is hard enough, but now you've also got bills, inventory, and repairs to keep up with!

Utilities

At the end of every week, there is a chance one of your utilities (gas, electricity, plumbing) shuts off, and it's your job to turn it back on by paying the bill.

If you ever finish a full week with no utilities active, your cafe is closed for repair, and you lose the game.

To turn a utility back on, just click on the faded icon. If you have enough money to pay for it, it will turn back on and your cafe will stay in business!

Upgrades

Having just opened, your cafe could still use some work. The two upgrade options found in the bottom right corner can be purchased at any point.

These upgrades provide you with a little context about what part of your cafe they'll improve, but they also come with drawbacks!

Whenever you purchase an upgrade, it will be swapped out with a new one for you to consider.

Restocking

After taking so many orders, you will start running low on ingredients. There's an easy fix to this!

At any point, you can click the "Buy" button beneath an ingredient to fully restock it to its maximum capacity.

Miscellaneous

Just a few more things to know before you begin your career as a cafe owner.

The Witch

Throughout your game, there is a chance that a witch enters your shop and offers you a deal.

She will explain the possible consequences of her spells, but it's up to you to decide if you can take the risk.

You can refuse her offers with no consequences. If you accept, she will tell you the effects of her spell. The witch will not offer you another spell until you either accept or refuse her current offer.

Stats

This game handles lots of variables, so it's best if you understand what each of them means.

Score: This is what you look at between playthroughs of this game to see how you did. Buying upgrades and serving customers increases it.

Prestige: Keep this value as high as you can. Serving customers increases it, but failing to serve a customer lowers your prestige. If prestige hits 0, your cafe closes!

Coins: This is how you pay for upgrades, utilities, and restocking ingredients. Serving customers gains you coins.

Tip Jar

If you're ever short on funds, you can take from the tip jar.

Click the Tip Jar icon to immediately gain money. There is no limit to the number of times you can do this.

And That's It!

Now that you know how to play, you're ready to open your cafe!
